Test converting a group from a mailing list to a forum
======================================================

   >>> browser = Browser.logIn()
   >>> browser.getLink('Moderatoriai').click()

   >>> browser.getLink('Edit').click()
   >>> browser.url
   'http://localhost/group/moderators/edit'

   >>> browser.getControl('Forum type').value
   ['mailinglist']

   >>> browser.getControl('Forum type').value = ['forum']
   >>> browser.getControl('Save').click()

   >>> browser.url
   'http://localhost/group/moderators/home'

   >>> browser.getLink('Home').click()
   >>> browser.url
   'http://localhost/home'

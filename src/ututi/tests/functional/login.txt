Log in form
===========

Our system initially comes with one user, who is also a super
user. Let's try logging in as him.

If user is not logged in login form is shown. Let's fill it in:

    >>> browser = Browser()
    >>> browser.open('http://localhost/')
    >>> form = browser.getForm('login_form')
    >>> form.getControl('Email').value = "admin@ututi.lt"
    >>> form.getControl('Password').value = "asdasd"
    >>> form.getControl('Login').click()

And we are greeted by the welcome page:

    >>> browser.printQuery('id("page-content")//h1/text()')
    Welcome Adminas Adminovix!

The personal information portlet shows my name:

    >>> browser.printQuery('id("personal-box")//span[@class="fullname"]/text()')
    Adminas Adminovix

After login we are redirected to our home page.

    >>> browser.url
    'http://localhost/home'

As we have successfully logged in let's logout and make some typos:

    >>> browser.getLink('Log out').click()
    >>> browser.url
    'http://localhost/?came_from=http%3A%2F%2Flocalhost%2Fdologin'

If we enter the username wrong - we get redirected back to the login
form, but not logged in:

    >>> form = browser.getForm('login_form')
    >>> form.getControl('Email').value = "odmin@ututi.lt"
    >>> form.getControl('Password').value = "asdasd"
    >>> form.getControl('Login').click()

    >>> browser.url
    'http://localhost/'

Let's enter the password wrong:

    >>> form = browser.getForm('login_form')
    >>> form.getControl('Email').value = "admin@ututi.lt"
    >>> form.getControl('Password').value = "asd"
    >>> form.getControl('Login').click()

    >>> browser.url
    'http://localhost/'

Still permission is denied.

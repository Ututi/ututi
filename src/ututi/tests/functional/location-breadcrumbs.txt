Location breadcrumbs
===================

Test if location portion of the breadcrumbs is rendered correctly.


Log in first.

    >>> browser = Browser.logIn()

The subject:

    >>> browser.open('http://localhost/subject/uni/mat_analize')
    >>> browser.printQuery('id("BreadLinks")//li/a/text()')
    uni

The group:

    >>> browser.open('http://localhost/group/moderators')
    >>> browser.printQuery('id("BreadLinks")//li/a/text()')
    vu

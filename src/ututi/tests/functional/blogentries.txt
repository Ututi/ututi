Blog entries
============

Test the administration of blog entries.
     >>> browser = Browser.logIn()
     >>> browser.open('http://localhost/admin/blog')

     >>> browser.getLink('New entry').click()

     >>> browser.getControl('Date').value = '2009-10-10'
     >>> browser.getControl('Content').value = 'Find stuff to read on our blog!'
     >>> browser.getControl('Save').click()

     >>> browser.url
     'http://localhost/admin/blog'

     >>> browser.printQuery('id("entry_list")//li/text()')
     Find stuff to read on our blog!

     >>> browser.getLink('Edit').click()
     >>> browser.getControl('Content').value = 'Find more stuff to read on our blog!'
     >>> browser.getControl('Update').click()

     >>> browser.printQuery('id("entry_list")//li/text()')
     Find more stuff to read on our blog!
